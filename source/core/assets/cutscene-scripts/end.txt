The drones spiralled towards the ground, rotors ceasing to spin.
Blinking LEDs all around the room briefly flickered before fizzling out completely.
The central AI, once suspended in the air, violently crashed into the ground, sending debris flying in all directions.
A great, deafening silence filled the room. Not even the faintest electrical hum could be heard.
I knew at that moment that I had won - humanity had one.
No matter how long it took, no matter how difficult, we would rebuild.
